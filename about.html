Who We Are?

A2Z is a family owned, retail and wholesale plant nursery, located in Miami, FL. We are committed to providing landscapers, garden designers, horticulturists and homeowners, the highest quality plants at unbeatable prices. We provide you with the necessary knowledge to properly care and maintain your plants. Our leading team of experts is ready to help you obtain the best overall experience in your upcoming plant adventure. Our mission is to support you throughout the process of selecting the right plant for you. We grow a remarkable array – more than 35 varieties – of trees, shrubs, grasses and ferns in a controlled environment. We carefully select each plant with the highest quality standards, to ensure it arrives in perfect conditions. Offering detailed care instructions, of either your indoor plants or outdoor garden plants. Our goal is to help you achieve the garden of your dreams. Master the ways of Ring a Bell Farms so they may bloom into everything you dreamed of. Understanding your needs is our top priority.
